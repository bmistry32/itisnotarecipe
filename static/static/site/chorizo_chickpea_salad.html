CHORIZO CHICKPEA SALAD

Chorizo Chickpea Salad Meat

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Chorizo
  • 1 Large Red Onion
  • 2 Red Peppers
  • 1 Chickpeas Can
  • Herbs
  • Lettuce / Salad

Optional Ingredients

  • Sundried Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Cut up the chorizo into slices and fry in a pan for about 10m
  2. Take the chorizo out the pan and leave to the side
  3. Add in the chopped up onions and peppers and cook for another 10m
  4. Add in the herbs you are using
  5. Re-add the chorizo
  6. Add in the drained chickpeas
  7. If you're adding sundried tomatoes add them in after another 2m
  8. Mix in with your salad of choice
