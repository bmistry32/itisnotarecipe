APPLE PORK BURGER

Burger Pork Meat

Ingredients

  • 2 Red Apples
  • 5% Pork Mince
  • Breadcrumbs or porridge oats
  • Cumin
  • Cinnamon
  • Paprika

Instructions

  1. Combine pork mince with spices (cumin, cinammon and paprika)
  2. Grate the apples and add it into the mix
  3. Add in the breadcrumbs/oats
  4. Mix everything together well until you get one big ball of mince
  5. Split the ball into smaller balls of mince, and flatten them into a burger
  6. Place the raw burget patties onto a hot oiled pan and fry. Be sure to flip over after a side is done.
