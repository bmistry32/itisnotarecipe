TOFU STIR FRY

Tofu Stir Fry Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Block Of Tofu
  • Noodles
  • Garlic
  • Honey
  • Soy Sauce
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Tomatoes
  • Birds Eye Chilli
  • Any Stir Fry Vegetables
  • Cornflour

Optional Ingredients

  • Sesame Seeds

Instructions

  1. Put oil in the pan
  2. Add sesame seeds and garlic
  3. Add in tomatoes, salt, paprika and cumin
  4. Leave to cook for a bit
  5. Add in diced chillies
  6. Add in all of your diced vegetables
  7. Add Noodles in once vegetables are nicely cooked
  8. Mix soy sauce and Shallow Red Wine Vinegar together
  9. Pour sauce into mixture and let it stew
  10. Once the sauce has simmered away, serve the stir fry and place the fried tofu on top

Second part of dish

  1. Cut Tofu into blocks
  2. Make sauce of Soy Sauce and Honey
  3. Dunk Tofu into it and leave it to sit for a while
  4. Add in Cornflour and mix till you get a weird mixture
  5. Put Tofu into a hot pan and let it cook
  6. Flip over after about 10m
  7. Leave to cook for another 10m before taking it out
