MEATBALL BLACK BEAN CHILLI

Pork Black Beans Meat Vegetarian Alternative Possible

Ingredients

  • 5% Pork Mince - 1/2 pack
  • 1 Can Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 Can Black Beans (Works with kidney also)
  • Chipotle Paste
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Yellow Pepper
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Ground Cumin
  • Spring Onions
  • Cinammon
  • Breadcrumbs / oats
  • Garlic
  • Stock cube

Optional Ingredients

  • Avocado

Instructions

  1. Put the onion and peppers in the pot and allow to fry in oil
  2. Add in Garlic, Paprika and Cumin
  3. Once veg is soft, add in a table spoon of Chipotle Paste
  4. Mix together nicely
  5. Add stock in (cube + hot water already mixed or not), and mix well
  6. Add the fried meatballs into the pan
  7. Cover the pot and leave to cook for 10-15m
  8. Add in the canned tomates and beans
  9. Allow to cook for a further 10m
  10. Server, add chunks of avocado if you want.

Second part of dish

  1. To make the meatballs...
  2. Mix the Pork mince with Cumin, Cinammon and Paprika
  3. Add in breadcrumbs/oats
  4. Add in Spring onions chopped finely
  5. Mix all together into large ball then split up small bits and roll into smaller balls (12 or so)
  6. Fry in a hot oiled pand and turn balls when they are browned on one side
