FRIED AUBERGINE AND SALAD

Mediterranean Salad Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 Aubergine
  • 1 Pack of Feta
  • 1/2 Large Red Onion
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Lemon Juice
  • 2 Vine Ripened Tomatoes

Optional Ingredients

  • Basil Leaves

Instructions

  1. Cut Aubergine in half
  2. Score each half of the aubergine
  3. Rub in salt and olive oil
  4. Rub in black pepper and a herb of choice (parsley)
  5. Add butter to the frying pan
  6. Once butter is somewhat melted, put Aubergine halves in (skin down)
  7. Wait 10m or so and flip it over
  8. Wait 10m or so and flip it over again

Second part of dish

  1. Cut red onion into fine strips
  2. Mix in Vinegar
  3. Cut and add in the tomatoes
  4. Mix in lemon juice
  5. Leave to sit until Aubergine is cooked
  6. Add in cubes of Feta
